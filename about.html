I’m Miki Inada, a web developer from Japan based in Vancouver🇨🇦. I moved to Vancouver in 2017, fulfilling my dream of working at a local café while immersing myself in English language studies. My journey into web development began with my work experience in Japan.

I started at a social networking app company, where I gained insight into technology and discovered a growing interest in it. Later, I transitioned to a role at a beauty product company as a management strategy and Systems administrator, where I contributed to the release of an online system by doing some coding. This experience sparked my passion for coding, inspiring me to pursue web development as a career.

Now, I’m thoroughly enjoying learning web development and expanding my skills in creating dynamic, user-friendly websites. I’m excited to continue building my expertise and contributing to meaningful projects in the tech world.
